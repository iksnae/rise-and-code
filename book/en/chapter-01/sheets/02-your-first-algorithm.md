## WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

An **algorithm** is just a fancy word for "step-by-step instructions." You make algorithms all the time without even knowing it. This activity shows you how to write one clearly.

---

## EXAMPLE: Making Tea

Here's an algorithm for making tea:

1. Get a cup
2. Fill the cup with water
3. Boil the water
4. Put a tea bag in the cup
5. Pour the hot water over the tea bag
6. Wait 3-5 minutes
7. Remove the tea bag
8. Add milk or sugar if you want
9. Drink it!

**That's an algorithm.**

It's not complicated. It's just: "Here's how to do this, step by step."

## **WHAT HAPPENED?**

If they succeeded great job!

If it was confusing, that's OK. That's the whole point. This shows you:
- Where your instructions were unclear
- What you forgot to mention
- How computers feel when instructions aren't clear enough

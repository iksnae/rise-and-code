# Chapter 7: Level Up

**Ready to test your skills? Coding challenges are like puzzles for your brain. They're tricky, they're fun, and they teach you something new every time. Let's go!**

## What You'll Learn

- How to solve programming problems systematically
- How to break complex challenges into smaller steps
- How to use hints when you get stuck
- How to debug (fix) your work
- How to learn from different solutions

## Sections

1. Try the Challenges
2. Get Hints
3. Check Your Work

## Activities

- **Beginner Challenges**: Five foundational challenges with step-by-step guidance
- **Intermediate Challenges**: Five medium-difficulty challenges combining multiple concepts
- **Advanced Challenges**: Five complex challenges for problem-solving mastery
- **Debugging Exercises**: Five challenges with intentional bugs to fix
- **Multiple Perspectives Exercises**: Five exercises to solve problems different ways

## PART 4: Using AND, OR, NOT

Now combine questions using Logic's tools.

**Scenario: Should I go outside?**

**YES/NO questions:**
- Is it sunny? YES
- Do I have free time? YES
- Do I want to play? YES

**Using AND:**  
"Is it sunny AND do I have free time?" = YES (because both are YES)

**Using OR:**  
"Is it sunny OR is it fun outside?" = YES (because at least one is YES)

**Your turn:**

**Scenario: Should I eat lunch?**

**YES/NO questions to ask:**
1. Am I hungry? ___
2. Is there food? ___
3. Do I have time? ___

**Using AND:**  
"Am I hungry AND is there food?" = ___

**Using OR:**  
"Am I hungry OR is it lunchtime?" = ___

**Using NOT:**  
"Am I NOT hungry?" = ___ (flip your hungry answer)
